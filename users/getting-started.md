# Users: Getting Started

## Accessing your dashboard
- Log in with the email tied to your store account.
- Confirm the store name shown in the header matches the location you shop with.
- Review alerts for pricing changes, pending approvals, or required actions.

## Browsing the collection
- Open the Collection page to explore available items.
- Use filters or search to narrow by category, brand, or condition.
- Save favorites to keep track of items you want to revisit.

## Staying informed
- Check notifications for trade-in updates or messages from your store.
- Update contact details so the store can reach you if needed.
- Enable email or SMS alerts in Settings to avoid missing updates.
