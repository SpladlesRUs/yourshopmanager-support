# Users: Trade-Ins & History

## Submitting a trade-in
- Use Trade In to request a valuation or pricing update.
- Provide clear item details (title, edition, condition notes) to speed up review.
- Upload photos if the store requests them for verification.

## Tracking status
- Visit Trade History to see pending, approved, or completed trades.
- Open a trade entry to view notes, offered price, and next steps.
- Watch for store feedback or additional info requests.

## Managing follow-ups
- If a trade is delayed, message the store via your preferred support channel.
- Keep receipts or reference numbers handy for quick lookup.
- Confirm pickup/drop-off instructions before finalizing the trade.
