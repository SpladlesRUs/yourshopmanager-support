# Users: Shopping List

## Building a list
- Add items you want to review in-store or track for future purchases.
- Include notes (condition preferences, price target) to help staff assist you.
- Reorder items so your highest-priority picks stay at the top.

## In-store assistance
- Share your list with staff to speed up item retrieval.
- Confirm availability before visiting, especially for limited stock.
- Update the list as your needs change.
