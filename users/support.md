# Users: Getting Help

## Contacting your store
- Reach out to your store administrator for account-specific requests.
- Use your store's preferred support channel for urgent issues.
- Provide your account email, trade ID, or order number when relevant.

## Submitting a ticket
- Email [support@hortonpress.com](mailto:support@hortonpress.com) to submit a ticket.
- Include screenshots and a short description of what you were doing.
- Expect a response within 48 hours.
