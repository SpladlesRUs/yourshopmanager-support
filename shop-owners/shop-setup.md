# Shop Owners: Shop Setup

## Branding and contact details
- Update logos, store name, and contact information in Admin > Shop Info.
- Verify operating hours and pickup/delivery policies.
- Add a support email or phone number for customer inquiries.

## Payments and launch readiness
- Confirm payment gateway settings before going live.
- Run a test transaction to validate receipts and payouts.
- Review tax settings and any required regional configurations.
